What is IRL?

IRL is the foundation of the IRL Robotics brand. While commonly known as “in real life,” within robotics it reflects a focus on real-world environments, real-world behavior, and real-world challenges that next-generation robots must learn to navigate.

IRL in Robotics

As robotics advances, especially in the areas of humanoids and general-purpose robots, the need for realistic and diverse datasets becomes critical. IRL represents the idea that robots should learn from real environments rather than relying solely on simulation or synthetic data.

This perspective influences how IRL Robotics approaches the broader robotics ecosystem: grounding development in practical, real-world conditions.

IRL as a Brand Identity

Within IRL Robotics, the term IRL symbolizes:

  • Real-world robotics over purely simulated approaches
  • Real environments that robots must navigate safely and intelligently
  • Real datasets that support the development of humanoids and emerging robotic systems

IRL is more than an acronym — it represents a direction, a philosophy, and a commitment to robotics that interacts meaningfully with the physical world.
